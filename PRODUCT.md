# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
High-end automotive enthusiasts, supercar collectors, and digital performance aficionados who expect a premium, high-octane experience.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Velox Auto's unyielding commitment to speed, design, and engineering excellence through a highly immersive, interactive digital showroom.

## Brand Personality
Aggressive, Elegant, Futuristic

## Anti-references
Cluttered SaaS dashboards, generic templates, slow-loading pages, and overly bright/friendly interfaces.

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Let the 3D models and animations communicate performance.
- **Motion Implies Speed**: Use precise, eased animations to convey high engineering standards.
- **Expert Confidence**: Keep copy minimal and bold; the product speaks for itself.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast for readability, support reduced-motion preferences, and ensure keyboard navigability for core configurator interactions.
